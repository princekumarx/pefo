Quickly Create Portfolio Company

You can enter basic details of Portfolio Company through Quick create. This might be helpful if you are running out of time.
Steps to create Portfolio Company through Quick create
  1. Click Quick Create drop-down at the top right.
  2. Choose New Portfolio.
  3. In the Create Portfolio view, fill in the details.
  4. Click Save.
