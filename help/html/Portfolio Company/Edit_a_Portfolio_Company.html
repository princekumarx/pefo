Edit a Portfolio Company

To Edit a Portfolio Company
  1. Click on Portfolio Company option in the main menu.
  2. In the Portfolio Company list view, click edit under action.
  3. You will see edited view of Portfolio Information.
  4. Make desired changes in the fields and click Save.
