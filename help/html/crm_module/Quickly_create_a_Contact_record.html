Quick Create

You can enter basic details of Contact record through Quick create. This might be helpful if you are running out of time.

Steps to create Contact records through Quick create

  1. Click Quick Create drop-down at the top right corner.
  2. Choose New Contact.
  3. In the Create Contact view, fill in the Contact details.
  4. Click Save.
