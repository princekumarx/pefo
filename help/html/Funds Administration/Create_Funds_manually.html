What is a Fund?

Fund is a collective investment scheme which pools money from Investors for making investments in Portfolio companies.


Create Fund

To create fund

  1. Click on Funds in the main menu.
  2. Click on Create Fund Icon () at the top.
  3. You will see Create New Fund window.
  4. Fill at least the required fields and click Save.

  5. Fund will be created and a new window will appear.
