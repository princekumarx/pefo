Quick Create Investor

You can enter basic details of Investor through Quick create. This might be helpful if you are running out of time.
Steps to create Investor through Quick create
  1. Click Quick Create drop-down at the top right.
  2. Choose New Investor.
  3. In the Create Investor view, fill in the Investor details.
  4. Click Save.
