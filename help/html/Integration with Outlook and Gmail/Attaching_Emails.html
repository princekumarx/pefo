Attaching Emails

You can select an email and attach it to corresponding record in a selected module in PE Front Office. You can attach emails to Contacts and also associate them to a Deal, Portfolio or Investor.


Follow few simple steps to attach an email to corresponding record in PE Front Office

  1. Select desired email
  2. Click on Attach Email
  3. Click Select Fields from Contacts to search against different fields of Contacts
  4. Email id from the selected email will be populated in the search records text space. You can edit it and provide desired search criteria. Click Search PEFO CRM
