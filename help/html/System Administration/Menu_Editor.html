Menu Editor

With the help of Menu Editor, you can have most frequently accessed modules on your menu bar. This will ensure easy navigation, and switching between different modules is easy. Also, you can shuffle their positions according to your requirements.


Follow few simple steps to configure desired modules on menu bar

  1. Click gear icon in the upper right.
  2. Click CRM Settings.
  3. Click Menu Editor under Studio.
  4. All active modules are available for selection. Select desired modules from list of modules.
  5. Change the arrangement of the modules using the up and down arrow keys.
  6. Click Save.
  7. Refresh your page to see desired items on your menu bar.


Notes! You can only select upto 10 modules to have on menu bar
