This workflow requires code level changes and hence doesn't support on Sumantra CRM On-Demand accounts. Few custom functions are developed to meet specific requirements in Sumantra CRM such as :

  1. Quantity of products will be decreased when Invoice is generated on them.
  2. Quantity of products is increased when purchase order status is update to Received Shipment
  3. Email notification send to ticket owner and related contact when ticket is created, comments exchanged, and closed.
