I am going to send a email with deal details to assigned user or group.


1. Select Deal Pipeline in Select Module and my condition will be Until the first time the condition is true.



2. The idea is to send an alert exclusively to assigned user or group about the change in deal stage to screened. So, my condition is Deal Stage is Screened



3. Click on New Task button and select Send Mail. Provide Task Title; this is just for your reference. Status should be set Active. If you do not want this workflow to send emails anymore, you can make status In Active. In the To field, you can either select email variables from the dropdown next to it, or provide emails manually. My intention is to send emails to assigned user. I will select Email from the dropdown. And then, you can provide Subject which is actual subject of the email, and compose email body.

Sumantra CRM gives you the flexibility to select field variables which are displayed as field values when email is sent. For instance, the field variable $dealname will display actual name populated from Deal Name field of the record.



4. Click Save

5. Finally, click Save again as you complete the process of creating the workflow


Execute Task

Basically, workflow tasks send email within 15 mins after the conditions are met. However, you can also configure your workflow to send email for you in desired time in future.



Here's how to do it

  1. In the Send Email task, click on Execute Task check-box
  2. Provide the number of days after which the email should be triggered
  3. Select After from the second dropdown
  4. From the third dropdown, select Created Time or Modified Time. Your workflow is triggered depending upon the values in Created Time or Modified Time fields of the records in Sumantra CRM
